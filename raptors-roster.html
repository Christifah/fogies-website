Scottie Barnes (#4)

Scottie Barnes is a versatile forward with elite defensive instincts and playmaking ability. Drafted from Florida State, he’s the emotional leader of the team. His versatility allows him to guard multiple positions and initiate offense. Barnes is the face of the Raptors’ rebuild.

RJ Barrett (#9)

RJ Barrett is a Canadian-born wing known for his scoring and toughness. A Duke alum, he brings experience and poise to the Raptors lineup. Barrett thrives in isolation and transition. He’s a key piece in Toronto’s long-term plans.
