Scottie Barnes (#4)

Scottie Barnes is a versatile forward known for his energy and leadership. He was drafted from Florida State and quickly became a fan favorite. His defense and playmaking make him a cornerstone of the Raptors’ future. Off the court, he’s known for his infectious positivity and team-first attitude.

RJ Barrett (#9)

RJ Barrett is a proud Canadian and a dynamic scorer. He played college basketball at Duke and brings toughness and clutch shooting to the Raptors. RJ is a key part of the team’s offensive strategy. His leadership and experience help guide younger players on the roster.

Immanuel Quickley (#5)

Immanuel Quickley is a speedy point guard known for his shooting and hustle. He played at Kentucky and has become a reliable contributor in the backcourt. Quickley’s ability to create plays and score in bunches makes him a fan favorite. He’s also praised for his work ethic and leadership.
